The cover art for the album was created by Rachel Cook. She is a Junior Art Major at the University of Georgia. Examples of her work can be found at http://www.flickr.com/photos/raeward.

The album's graphic design was created by Andy Whitehead, co-owner of Hot Route Records.

Kalindi Winfield collaborated on the album's graphic design.

Eve's story on the inside booklet was written by Kalindi Winfield, with contributions from Maggie Packer, Rosemary Heath, Liz Ely-Kelso, Hannah Baker, Katie O'toole.

The poem opposite the CD tray was written by Sameer Vashisht, co-owner of Hot Route Records. It is entitled "Relevance".
