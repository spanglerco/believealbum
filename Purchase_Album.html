To purchase your copy of the album, you have three options:

If you live in Athens, the Belie-v-e album is being sold at these fine establishments:

  1. Jittery Joes Coffee (Baxter, 5-Points, and Student Learning Center locations)
  2. Add Drug Store (next to Earthfare in 5 points)
  3. The Homeplace (across the street from Add Drug Store in 5 points)
  4. Wuxtry (record store on Clayton Street, downtown)
  5. Schoolkids Records (record store on Clayton Street, downtown)

Additionally, you can order the album from Athensmusic.net.

Finally, we hope to have online ordering via Paypal soon.
