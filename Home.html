The Belie-v-e Album is Athens' response to the tragic loss of a model citizen, Eve Carson. She taught us all how to laugh and enjoy every part of life, and continues to guide us still. A handful of Athenians organized a compilation cd, featuring local musicians, as well as spoken word from Eve's former teachers. The bands include Bain Mattox, the Connelly Crowe Band, Blue Flashing Light, and the Modern Skirts.

On August 17th, we released the album at the Melting Point, and hosted 400 guests who wanted to relive Eve's magic, and celebrate her spirit and enthusiasm. The night raised over $6,000 for the Foundation for Excellence, which will use all of the CD's proceeds to start a scholarship in Eve's name. But our work is not over, we have many more album's to sell for this great cause. If you would like to purchase a CD, visit the Purchase Album page.
